> # My Favorite Restaurant

Well, that's easy.  My favorite restaurant is Cuca's Mexican Cantina in Redlands, California.  Their secret is fresh ingredients, and grandma's recipes.  Some of my favorites are: 

* veggie burrito
* veggie taco
* rice and beans
* veggie fajitas

To get there, you have to:
1. Take I-10 to the Redlands exit
2. Turn _right_ on Redlands Blvd.
3. Turn _left_ on Ochoa
4. Turn _right_ on 12th

It will be on your _right_.
